SOLAR PANELS

Explore the next generation of solar photovoltaic systems for your home.

Overview

Solar panels on your roof can convert the sun's rays into electricity for your home. These panels are made of solar cells, usually from silicon, that can generate power through the photovoltaic effect. As sunlight hits your panels, the solar cells convert this light directly into direct current electricity. Inverters then convert this to alternating current that can power your home's appliances and devices. Solar energy produced during the day offsets the electricity you would otherwise buy from the grid. And if your solar panels generate more electricity than your home uses, this excess can go back into the utility grid.


Is It Right For You?

Solar panels can significantly reduce your electricity bills.

Your roof's orientation and position maximizes solar production.

High solar potential ensures that you'll get the most value out of your panels.

If your home meets these conditions, solar power is likely a smart long-term investment that can reduce your environmental impact and energy costs.

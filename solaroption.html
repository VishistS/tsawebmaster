SOLAR PANELS

The sun's boundless energy, made available for your home.

Overview

Solar panels allow you to harness the power of the sun for your home's energy needs. The panels, made from semiconductive material, work by converting the sun's rays into useable electricity for your home. Your solar panels can be mounted on the roof; placed in your backyard; or whereever the sun shines bright.

Roof Mounted Panels
Optimal Sun Exposure

Solar panels also integrate seamlessly with your home. The panels will produce electricity during the daytime, offsetting electricity usage. Any solar generated electricity that you don't use can be sold back to the grid, which means you could literally be making money off of your panels.


Is It Right For You?

Solar panels can significantly reduce your electricity bills.

Your roof's orientation and position maximizes solar production.

High solar potential ensures that you'll get the most value out of your panels.

If your home meets these conditions, solar power is likely a smart long-term investment that can reduce your environmental impact and energy costs.

Tax Information

The state of Texas does not specifically offer tax credits for homeowners with solar panels; however, the federal government does (woohoo). Until 2032, eligible homeowners can get up to 30% of the installation cost back as a credit on federal taxes. This means you could be getting thousands of dollars back from your taxes per year.

Our Story

A passion for sustainability since day one.

Darius, pictured here with his wife, Lila, in front of Green Eats, was a visionary gardener and animal rights advocate who transformed a small family farm into a beacon of sustainability in the 1990s. Inspired by their world travels and shared passion for ethical living, Darius began by sharing plant-based meals from their farmhouse kitchen with friends and neighbors. That didn't last very long; their use of fresh, locally grown ingredients combined with a lifestyle of compassion made Green Eats a popular vegan community-supported cafe. And the legacy lives on: sustaining a business that Darius and Lila dedicated to being sustainable, kind, and tasty.

Our Story

A passion for sustainability since day one.

Darius, pictured here with his son in front of Green Eats, was a visionary gardener and animal rights advocate who turned a small family farm into a beacon of sustainability in the 1990s. Inspired by his global travels and passion for ethical living, Darius started by sharing plant-based meals from his farmhouse kitchen with friends and neighbors. His dedication to fresh, locally grown ingredients and a compassionate lifestyle quickly gained a following, transforming Green Eats into a beloved community-supported vegan café. Today, this legacy lives on, honoring Darius’s commitment to sustainability, kindness, and delicious food.

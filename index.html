Our Story

Thirty years ago, Green Eats was born from a humble dream in a small coastal town. Its founder, a passionate gardener and animal rights advocate named John, transformed his family farm into a sanctuary for sustainable living. Inspired by his travels and the realization of how food connects humanity to the planet, he began serving hearty, plant-based meals to friends and neighbors in his rustic farmhouse kitchen. Word spread quickly, and his dedication to using fresh, locally grown ingredients blossomed into a community-supported vegan café. Today, Green Eats continues to honor its roots by championing the power of plants, sustainability, and compassion on every plate.

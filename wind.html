WIND TURBINES

Spin into a cleaner planet by harnessing Earth's natural winds.

Overview

Wind turbines convert wind energy into clean electricity, contributing to a greener planet. They minimize emissions, combat climate change, and provide scalable solutions for various scenarios. With these efficient, environmentally friendly constructions, you can embrace renewable energy, promote energy independence, and join the global movement toward sustainable living.

Vertical Wind Turbines

You may have heard of stereotypes surrounding the size of wind turbines; however, they can almost seamlessly integrate into your home! Modern, scalable turbines are suitable for residential and commercial sites, allowing for localized power generation. These buildings apply to both urban and rural locations, providing decentralized, energy-efficient solutions. Embrace wind power's aesthetic and functional appeal for a more sustainable future.

Tax Rebates

The state of Texas does not specifically offer tax credits for homeowners with wind turbines; however, the federal government does (woohoo). Until 2032, eligible homeowners can get up to 30% of the installation cost back as a credit on federal taxes. This means you could be getting thousands of dollars back from your taxes per year.
For more information and specific eligibility requirements - please refer to the IRS.
